## 💡 Why "Learning in Public"?

Writing something down forces me to actually understand it. If I can't explain a
step clearly, that's a signal I don't fully get it yet. Publishing openly also
means:

- **Accountability** — it keeps me consistent.
- **Feedback** — people smarter than me can point out where I'm wrong.
- **Giving back** — most of what I know, I learned from other people's notes.

If something here helps you, great. If you spot a mistake or a better approach,
even better — issues and pull requests are very welcome.
